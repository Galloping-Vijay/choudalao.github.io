PHP Ajax 跨域问题最佳解决方案

<p helvetica="" pingfang="" hiragino="" sans="" microsoft="" noto="" cjk="" wenquanyi="" micro="" color:="" white-space:="" background-color:="">本文通过设置<strong>Access-Control-Allow-Origin</strong>来实现跨域。</p><p helvetica="" pingfang="" hiragino="" sans="" microsoft="" noto="" cjk="" wenquanyi="" micro="" color:="" white-space:="" background-color:="">例如：客户端的域名是client.runoob.com，而请求的域名是server.runoob.com。</p><p helvetica="" pingfang="" hiragino="" sans="" microsoft="" noto="" cjk="" wenquanyi="" micro="" color:="" white-space:="" background-color:="">如果直接使用ajax访问，会有以下错误：</p><pre lay-lang="JavaScript"><code class="JavaScript">XMLHttpRequest cannot load http://server.runoob.com/server.php. No ‘Access-Control-Allow-Origin’ header is present on the requested resource.Origin ‘http://client.runoob.com’ is therefore not allowed access.</code></pre><h2 helvetica="" pingfang="" hiragino="" sans="" microsoft="" noto="" cjk="" wenquanyi="" micro="" white-space:="" background-color:=""><br></h2><h2>1、允许单个域名访问</h2><p><br></p><p helvetica="" pingfang="" hiragino="" sans="" microsoft="" noto="" cjk="" wenquanyi="" micro="" color:="" white-space:="" background-color:="">指定某域名（http://client.runoob.com）跨域访问，则只需在http://server.runoob.com/server.php文件头部添加如下代码：</p><pre lay-lang="JavaScript"><code class="JavaScript">header(‘Access-Control-Allow-Origin:http://client.runoob.com’);</code></pre><h2 helvetica="" pingfang="" hiragino="" sans="" microsoft="" noto="" cjk="" wenquanyi="" micro="" white-space:="" background-color:=""><br></h2><h2>2、允许多个域名访问</h2><p><br></p><p helvetica="" pingfang="" hiragino="" sans="" microsoft="" noto="" cjk="" wenquanyi="" micro="" color:="" white-space:="" background-color:="">指定多个域名（http://client1.runoob.com、http://client2.runoob.com等）跨域访问，则只需在http://server.runoob.com/server.php文件头部添加如下代码：</p><pre lay-lang="JavaScript"><code class="JavaScript">$origin = isset($_SERVER[‘HTTP_ORIGIN’]) ? $_SERVER[‘HTTP_ORIGIN’] : ‘’; $allow_origin = array( ‘http://client1.runoob.com’, ‘http://client2.runoob.com’); if (in_array($origin, $allow_origin)) { header(‘Access-Control-Allow-Origin:’ . $origin); }</code></pre><h2 helvetica="" pingfang="" hiragino="" sans="" microsoft="" noto="" cjk="" wenquanyi="" micro="" white-space:="" background-color:=""><br></h2><h2>3、允许所有域名访问</h2><p><br></p><p helvetica="" pingfang="" hiragino="" sans="" microsoft="" noto="" cjk="" wenquanyi="" micro="" color:="" white-space:="" background-color:="">允许所有域名访问则只需在http://server.runoob.com/server.php文件头部添加如下代码：</p><pre lay-lang="JavaScript"><code class="JavaScript">header(‘Access-Control-Allow-Origin:*’);</code></pre><p><br></p>
